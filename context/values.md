# About the Company

## About Figma: Vision and Mission
Vision: Make design accessible to all
Mission: Help teams collaborate visually 
## About Figma: Values
### Build Community
We're multiplayer people, and we love the weird, wonderful magic that happens when humans connect. We bring people together so they can bring new ideas to life.
**It means . . .**
1 Creating warm, inclusive spaces where everyone feels welcome
2 Building bridges between Figma and our users
3 Sharing creative projects and obsessions so we can inspire each other.

### Love Your Craft
We build for builders, and try to make complex things feel simple. In our products and process, we invest in the details few people see, but everyone feels.
**It means . . .**
1 Finding joy in the things we make
2 Paring everything down to its essence
3 Making thoughtful trade-offs between business goals and customer needs
4 Using first principles to clarify problems and rethink assumptions

### Run With It
When you see something that needs doing, do it. When you have a great idea, run with it. Tackle big, scary, exciting challenges like Figma's future depends on it. Because it does.
**It means . . .**
1 Taking initiative and being bold
2 Seeking feedback without getting paralyzed by input
3 Hitting deadlines and keeping things moving
